What are passkeys?

  • Passkeys replace passwords with biometrics or device PIN
  • Built on the WebAuthn standard, supported by all major browsers
  • Phishing-resistant — credentials are bound to the origin
  • No shared secrets — only a public key is stored on the server
  • Synced across your devices via your platform's password manager
